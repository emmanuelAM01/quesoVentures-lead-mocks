More than a bakery.

Eat Cake Bar is a scratch-made dessert destination in the Houston Heights. Every item on our menu is made from home-based recipes using only the finest local ingredients. From show-stopping custom cakes to our famous Chubby Cookies — this is the place for your next dessert craving.

We bring over 20 years of pastry experience to our storefront, having served hotels, restaurants, and private events long before opening our doors in 2021. Walk in and stay a while.
