A Neighborhood Bar Since Day One

Nico's Bar & Grill is located at 22610 Loop 494 in Kingwood, Texas. We're a full-service bar and grill with a covered outdoor patio, a live music stage, pool tables, and dart boards.

We serve stone-fired pizza and a full drink menu. There's something happening every night of the week — from live bands on Friday nights to karaoke on Thursdays.

We're open every day from 11:00 AM to 2:00 AM. Walk-ins are always welcome.
