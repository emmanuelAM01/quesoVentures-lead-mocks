Our Story

Straight From Ecuador
to Houston

Born from a deep love of Ecuadorian culture and tradition, our food truck brings the vibrant, bold flavors of Ecuador directly to the streets of Humble and Greater Houston. Every single dish is made from authentic family recipes passed down through generations.

Whether you're Ecuadorian and craving a taste of home, or discovering our cuisine for the very first time — every bite tells a story of flavor, culture, and pride.

What We Serve

Our Menu Favorites

Handcrafted with authentic Ecuadorian spices and techniques. Made fresh every single day.

🍗
Main Dish — Plato Principal
Seco de Pollo

Tender chicken slow-cooked in a rich Ecuadorian stew of beer, cilantro, tomato, and aji amarillo. Served with rice, fried plantains, and avocado.

🍤
Soup — Sopa
Encebollado

Ecuador's most iconic soup. Albacore tuna, yuca, tomato, cilantro, and pickled red onion in a bold broth. A national treasure in every bowl.

🥓
Appetizer / Side — Entrada
Llapingachos

Crispy pan-fried potato cakes stuffed with cheese, served with peanut sauce, fried egg, chorizo, and a fresh tomato curtido salad.

🥩
Main Dish — Plato Principal
Hornado de Chancho

Slow-roasted whole pork, crisped to perfection in Ecuadorian spices. Served with mote, llapingachos, and aji sauce. A true feast.

🦐
Ceviche — Mariscos
Ceviche de Camarón

Fresh shrimp cured in lime juice, tomato, onion, and cilantro. Served with chifles (plantain chips) and canguil. Bright, bold, refreshing.

🌿
Snack / Side — Bocadillo
Patacones con Todo

Twice-fried green plantain rounds, crispy on the outside, loaded with your choice of shrimp ceviche, pulled pork, or black bean & avocado.
