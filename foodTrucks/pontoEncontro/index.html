We're your neighborhood meeting point.

Ponto de Encontro — "the meeting point" — is community-first specialty coffee rooted right here in Humble. We care about where our beans come from, how they're grown, and who grew them. We just don't make a big deal about it, because good coffee should feel like home, not a lecture.
