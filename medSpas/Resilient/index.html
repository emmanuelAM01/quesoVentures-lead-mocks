Recovery Services Tailored to You

Every body is different. Our therapists customize each session to your goals — whether you're an athlete, a weekend warrior, or just dealing with everyday pain and stiffness.

Functional Assisted Stretch Therapy

A trained therapist guides you through a full range of motion, correcting imbalances and releasing tension that self-stretching can't reach. Ideal for chronic tightness, posture correction, and injury prevention.

Percussion Therapy

Functional assisted stretch therapy is a one-on-one guided stretching session where a trained therapist manually stretches your muscles and joints through their full range of motion. It improves flexibility, reduces muscle tension, corrects posture, and helps prevent injury — going far deeper than self-stretching alone.
We are located at 17904 W Lake Houston Pkwy, Unit 403, Humble, TX 77346 — conveniently serving the greater Houston area including Atascocita, Kingwood, and surrounding communities.
Percussion therapy uses a handheld device to deliver rapid, targeted vibrations into muscle tissue. It breaks up knots, reduces delayed-onset muscle soreness (DOMS), increases blood flow, and accelerates post-workout or post-injury recovery.
Yes. EMS is a safe, non-invasive therapy widely used in physical therapy and sports medicine. It uses low-level electrical currents to stimulate muscle contractions, reduce pain, decrease inflammation, and improve neuromuscular function. Our therapists will review your health history before any session.
Compression therapy uses sequential pneumatic sleeves on your legs or arms. The alternating pressure promotes lymphatic drainage, increases circulation, flushes metabolic waste like lactic acid, and significantly reduces muscle soreness and swelling after exercise or injury.
